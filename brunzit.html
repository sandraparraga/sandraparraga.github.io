Branding project

VOL, a concert hall in Barcelona, opens its doors to debate, female empowerment, experimentation and innovation. We have designed the branding and campaign for the VOL school: Brunzit. Brunzit means buzz in catalan, and we take it as a metaphor for those people who can’t be silent.

The design concept consists on blurring VOL's actual posters, applying Brunzit's isotype with small squares or abstractions that allow us to imagine what or who are these things buzzing around VOL.

Collaboration with Judit Agüera and Quim Casadó
