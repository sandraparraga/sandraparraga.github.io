Time'sApp

Time’s App makes visible exactly what we do with our time, to understand that the concept of “not having time” does not exist.
Irene España and I have designed an app that auto-generates your new routines according to your interests. No more feeling like you are wasting your time. “Time’s App for not having time”
The texts and illustrations are made by us.
