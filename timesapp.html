Surveillance Power

Through the buttons we click, the pages we visit, the things we download and the posts we like, we are defining our preferences as consumers.
This design object is the representation of digital control, it's a surveillance camera in the form of the "like" icon.

Media: 3D Printing, Web Development

This continuous surveillance is the work of corporate interest, which has used design as a key element to attract our attention, through practices such as infinite scroll, the architecture of choice or advertising.
As report of my research on this topic, I have designed a web with an "infinite scroll" effect.
